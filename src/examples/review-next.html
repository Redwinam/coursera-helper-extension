Rubric  评分标准

Argument Score (choose criteria for Topic 1 or Topic 2)
论点评分（选择主题 1 或主题 2 的标准）

Evidence Score: Did the student use quotations or examples from the reading assigned for the week to support their argument?
证据得分：学生是否使用了本周指定的阅读材料中的引言或例子来支持其论点？

Exposition Score: Evaluate the quality of the writing. The sentences should be grammatical, and the paragraphs should be well-organized. The conclusion should be clear. Please remember that many of your peers are non-native English speakers. The exposition section is intended to examine the writer's ability to convey his or her ideas clearly, organize an essay logically, and make a coherent argument. Grammatical or spelling errors that do not drastically prevent readability should not receive deductions.
阐述评分：评估写作质量。句子应语法正确，段落应组织良好。结论应清晰。请记住，您的许多同伴是非英语母语者。阐述部分旨在考察作者清晰表达其想法、逻辑组织文章以及构建连贯论点的能力。不应因不影响阅读的语法或拼写错误而扣分。

Overall evaluation/feedback: Are there any additional comments that you would like to make about this assignment?
总体评价/反馈：您对这项作业还有什么其他评论想要提出吗？
